Share Your Card

Show the QR code or tap Share to beam your card to anyone instantly.

Point camera at QR to open card

Tap Share via Nearby / NFC above, then choose Nearby Share in the Android share sheet — your phone will use NFC + Bluetooth to beam the card link to a nearby device.

On iPhone: Use the Share button above (AirDrop) or let the other person scan the QR code. Web NFC is only available on Android Chrome.

NFC Not Available

Web NFC tag writing requires Android Chrome on a device with NFC hardware. It is not supported on iOS, Firefox, or desktop browsers.
